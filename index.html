Arin Chambers

Arin laid her foundation in the humanities at Marymount Manhattan College and currently pursues a Master’s degree in Linguistics from Middle Tennessee State University. Her professional journey spans roles as a researcher, tutor, and barista, reflecting a dynamic skillset with relational curiosity. She offers years of experience in leadership, data organization, and service. She has lived in Appalachia, China, and New York, but Antioch will always be her home.

Arin offers creative insight from years of 3D modeling and Printing, writing, and music production. Her professional strengths are customer connection, communication, and computational thinking. She fosters thoughtful and supportive conditions in any environment.

Arin brings global awareness to her work with proficiency in English, Mandarin, and French. She values service and corresponds with incarcerated women, volunteers with garden centers, and forages for others. She is especially passionate about science fiction and science communication, fields where imagination and precision intersect.
